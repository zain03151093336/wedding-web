Chinese Wedding Culture

It is Chinese tradition for the bride to wear a red dress, called a qipao and to cover her face with a red veil on the wedding day. In Chinese culture, red symbolizes happiness, prosperity and good luck. ... Many modern brides also change into a fourth dress at the end of the ceremonies to send off their guests.
